Alerts & Telegram

Pick what you want to be notified about. PokeSnipr's data job checks every 6 hours and pushes matching alerts to your Telegram.

1 · Connect Telegram

Not connected
  1. In Telegram, open @BotFather, send /newbot, and follow the prompts. Copy the bot token it gives you.
  2. Send any message to your new bot so it can reply to you.
  3. Open @userinfobot and copy your numeric chat ID.
  4. Paste both below. They're used only to build your setup — they're never committed to the site.

2 · Choose your alerts

Notify when any tracked card moves at least % day-over-day.

Notify when a sealed product (booster box, ETB, bundle…) moves at least % since the last check.

Notify when new PokeBeach / TCGplayer / Pokémon Center headlines are published.

Notify when a watchlisted card crosses a price you set. Add triggers from your watchlist below.

3 · Activate

Your choices are saved in this browser instantly. To turn on Telegram delivery, the settings below need to live in the site repo. Copy this config and commit it to config/alerts.json (or send it to whoever manages the site) — then add your bot token and chat ID as the GitHub secrets TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID.
