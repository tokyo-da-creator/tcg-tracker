1 · Push Notifications

Tap to enable

Get restock alerts and big price moves pushed directly to your phone — even when PokeSnipr isn't open. Works on iPhone (add to home screen first) and Android.

iPhone Tap Share → Add to Home Screen, then open the PokeSnipr app icon and enable below.
Android Just tap Enable — notifications work immediately in Chrome.

2 · Telegram Alerts

Not connected

Alternative to push notifications — configure a personal Telegram bot to receive alerts. Requires setting two GitHub secrets.

  1. In Telegram, open @BotFather, send /newbot, and follow the prompts. Copy the bot token it gives you.
  2. Send any message to your new bot so it can reply to you.
  3. Open @userinfobot and copy your numeric chat ID.
  4. Add both as GitHub repository secrets: TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID.

3 · What to alert

Notify when monitored products (Prismatic Evolutions ETB, Surging Sparks, etc.) come back in stock at Pokémon Center, Target, or Walmart. Checked every 30 minutes.

Notify when any tracked card moves at least % day-over-day.

Notify when a sealed product (booster box, ETB, bundle…) moves at least % since the last check.

Notify when new PokeBeach / TCGplayer / Pokémon Center headlines are published.

Notify when a watchlisted card crosses a price you set. Add triggers from your watchlist below.
